Identity, Culture & Heritage in South African Higher Education

Exploring the role of cultural identity and heritage in academic contexts

Context

South African higher education operates within a complex landscape of cultural diversity, historical legacy, and ongoing transformation. The role of identity, culture, and heritage in academic spaces continues to evolve as institutions work toward decolonization, inclusivity, and cultural responsiveness.
